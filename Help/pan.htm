The Pan tool enables users to navigate the map display by a simple click and drag of the mouse.

ðClick the Navigate Tab and select the Pan option to activate the tool  
ðHolding down the left mouse button, drag the map display in the appropriate direction and then release  
 
GeoPrise.NET will then regenerate a new map for the selected area.  

Tip: This functionality can also be accessed by selecting the tool from the Standard Toolbar.
